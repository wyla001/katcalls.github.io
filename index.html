All cancellations must happen 48 hours in advance of the appointment date. If the appointment is cancelled after this, the client will incur a 50% charge of the service they booked. In the incident that a client does not show up to their appointment with no prior notice, 100% of the service will be charged.
Apres Gel Nail Extensions are entirely made out of gel and soak off similarly to gel polish. These nails are thinner and more natural looking than the traditional acrylic nail. Additionally, acrylic nails are typically an extension added to the tip of the nail, whereas Apres Nails are full tip extensions placed over the entire nail bed, allowing for more natural growth underneath.
